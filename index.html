Faq

As a hair stylist, I frequently receive inquiries from clients about their hair services, pricing, and the process of booking an appointment. To help address these common questions, I’ve compiled a list of frequently asked questions. If you have any additional inquiries, please don’t hesitate to reach out to me!

What hair services do you offer?

I offer a wide range of hair services, including haircuts, coloring, highlights, balayage, treatments, and styling for special occasions such as weddings and events. If you have a specific style in mind, feel free to ask!

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by contacting me through email or social media. I recommend scheduling your appointment in advance, especially for weekends and holidays.

What is your cancellation or rescheduling policy?

If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please contact me at least 24 hours in advance. This allows me to offer the time slot to another client. Cancellations made with less notice may incur a fee.

Do you offer pre-appointment consultations?

Yes! I offer free consultations to discuss your desired look, assess your hair type, and provide personalized recommendations. Consultations can be scheduled in person or via phone before your appointment.

What products do you use?

I use high-quality professional products from trusted brands that are suitable for various hair types and concerns. If you have specific product preferences or allergies, please let me know, and I can accommodate your needs.

How often should I get a haircut or color?

The frequency of haircuts and coloring can vary based on your hair type and desired look. Generally, haircuts are recommended every 4-8 weeks, while color maintenance depends on the type of color and your hair growth. I can provide personalized recommendations during your appointment.

How to get exact hair styling price?​

- Send me an email that you find in the contact section
- I will send you possible dates for the consultation
- After the first consultation, I will be able to send you the exact price depending on our agreement
